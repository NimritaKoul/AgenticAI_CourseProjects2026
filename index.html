Course Overview

Welcome to the Agentic AI course repository! This space serves as the central hub for all project-related assignments, rubrics, and team information. Throughout the semester, students will ideate, propose, and implement cutting-edge agentic AI systems.

Project Rubrics

Evaluation Criteria

Projects are evaluated based on technical complexity (agentic framework design), robustness of reasoning and tool-use, creativity, and final presentation.

Grading breakdown

  • Proposal: 15%
  • Mid-term Review: 25%
  • Final Implementation: 40%
  • Documentation & Presentation: 20%
